## 📋 **Presentation Tips**

### **Before the Presentation**:
1. **Practice multiple times** - Time yourself (aim for 20-25 minutes)
2. **Test all demos** - Ensure everything works smoothly
3. **Prepare backup slides** - For common questions
4. **Check equipment** - Projector, internet, audio
5. **Have handouts ready** - Technical details, contact info

### **During the Presentation**:
1. **Start strong** - Hook the audience with the problem
2. **Tell a story** - Connect features to real benefits
3. **Show, don't just tell** - Live demos are powerful
4. **Engage the audience** - Ask questions, make eye contact
5. **Handle Q&A confidently** - Be prepared, admit what you don't know

### **Technical Demo Preparation**:
1. **Have stable environment** - Use production or reliable staging
2. **Prepare demo scenarios** - User registration, booking, admin dashboard
3. **Backup plans** - Screenshots, video recordings
4. **Focus on user benefits** - Not just technical features
5. **Keep it concise** - 2-3 key demos maximum

### **Common Questions to Prepare For**:
- How does this compare to existing solutions?
- What's your business model?
- How do you handle data privacy?
- What are your scalability plans?
- How do you ensure security?
- What's your go-to-market strategy?

### **Follow-up Actions**:
1. **Collect feedback** - Survey, direct conversations
2. **Share materials** - Slides, documentation, contact info
3. **Schedule follow-ups** - With interested parties
4. **Update project** - Based on feedback received
5. **Document lessons learned** - For future presentations
